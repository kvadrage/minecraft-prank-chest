# Future Ideas

## Prank Chest Variations

- **Upgrade Chest** — opposite of Prank Chest, upgrades items (rare recipe, maybe with Nether Star)
- **Random Chest** — 50/50 chance to upgrade or downgrade
- **Void Chest** — items slowly disappear one by one
- **Swap Chest** — items randomly shuffle between slots

## Social Features

- **Troll notification** — optional chat message: "[Player] just got pranked!" when someone opens a chest with downgraded items
- **Prank counter** — scoreboard tracking total pranks per player
- **Prank immunity** — crafted talisman that protects your items

## Advanced Mechanics

- **Chain reaction** — if a Prank Chest is placed next to a regular chest, items might "migrate"
- **Cumulative downgrade** — leaving items longer causes multiple downgrades
- **Configurable chains** — let server admins define custom downgrade mappings
